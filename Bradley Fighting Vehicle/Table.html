Bradley Variants
Model Name Year Introduced Uses
M2A2 Infantry Fighting Vehicle 1983 main mission is to provide protected
transport of an infantry squad (up to six passengers) to critical points.
M3A3 Cavalry Fighting Vehicle 1987 virtually identical to the M2 Bradley
except that it is equipped as a cavalry/scout vehicle. Instead of holding six
infantrymen in the payload compartment, it is designed to seat two scouts and hold additional radios and ammunition
M4C2V M4 Command and Control Vehicle 1994 designed to provide an automated tactical command post and operations center
M2A2 ods Warhammer Bradley 1999 the TOW missile system replaced with a two-tube Javelin Missile System, and ISU (Integrated Sight Unit)
modifications for increased anti-tank lethality
M2A2 ods M6 Linebacker 1990 An air defense variant, these vehicles are modified M2A2 ODSs with the TOW missile system replaced with a four-tube Stinger missile system
